Code. Design. Create. Climb. I'm really interested in forming a connection with technology, and it is totally a cliche and a buzzword-y turn of phrase to say this but I believe that someday apps will make us better artists, scientists, cooks, writers, and runners. What else matters in life?!

Hmm. These days I'm learning apple's UIKit and iOS frameworks, and iterating obsessively over my current app, SongBrush. I graduated from Brandeis last year with an M.A in computer science and took about a year to decide what to do with my life. I decided...that I should follow my wildest dreams, and get a boring 9-5 office development job while illustrating comics and exploring creative writing in my spare time. Then I discovered Swift and I latched onto the notion that software can create a relationship between user and machine, and here I am!
